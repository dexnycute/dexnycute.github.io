Why Gender Equality

Gender equality otherwwise knowns as sexual equality of the sexes is the state of equal ease of access to resources and opportunities. It can also be seen as when men and women enjoy the same right and opportunity across the sectors of society. Gender equality benefits everyone which is why it has been nominated as the united nation sustainable development goal (UN-SDG)number 5. As an organisation working towards brighter futures for all individuals. Gender equality is not just the concern of half of the world’s population; it is a human right, a concern for us all, because no society can develop – economically, politically, or socially – when half of its population is marginalized. We must leave no one behind..
